The boilerplate comes with a production-ready SEO suite designed to maximize your visibility.

## Key Features

- **Automated Sitemaps**: Your content is automatically indexed and reachable.
- **Granular Control**: Hide specific pages (like drafts or internal tools) from search results using `noindex`.
- **Structured Data**: Rich JSON-LD schemas (Organization, Article, FAQ) are pre-configured.
- **RSS Feeds**: Automatic feed generation for your blog and changelog.
- **Open Graph**: Beautiful social sharing cards generated automatically.
