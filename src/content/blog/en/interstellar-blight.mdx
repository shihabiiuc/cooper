The villain in Interstellar is not an alien or a robot - it is nature.

## Nitrogen Breathing

The blight was not just killing crops; it was consuming oxygen. Humanity was suffocating.

## Dust Bowls

The imagery of dust storms references the 1930s Dust Bowl, grounding the sci-fi nightmare in historical reality.
